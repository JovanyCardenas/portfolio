About Me

Hello! My name is Jovany Cardenas Vargas, and I am years old. I come from a family of two younger brothers and two older half-brothers. My parents are from Mexico and work in the strawberry fields. I was born and raised in Santa Maria, CA, and developed an interest in Computer Science during high school, starting with a Web Design class that sparked my passion for coding.

I graduated from Pioneer Valley High School in 2022 with a 3.6 GPA and continued my education at Allan Hancock College, where I am pursuing an Associate's in Science for Computer Science. My coursework includes CS111 (Fundamentals of Programming 1), CS112 (Fundamentals of Programming 2), CS131 (Computer Organization), and CS161 (Discrete Structures). I plan to transfer to a four-year university like CalPoly SLO to further my education and get my bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Outside my academic pursuits, I am involved in various extracurricular activities, including SkillsUSA, the Computer Science Club, the Science and Engineering Club, and support programs like EOPS, CAN-TRIO, and MESA. I have achieved significant success in SkillsUSA, winning gold at the state level for two years and silver at the national level. I also plan to go compete in SkillsUSA for a 3rd year, as well as being the President for SkillsUSA at Allan Hancock College.

In addition to being a full-time student, I work at the Career Center at Allan Hancock College as a student worker, assisting students with job searches, resume creation, typing tests, and meeting with career counselors. I also help organize events like Career Expo and Career Carnival, and support various programs within the Career Center, including SkillsUSA and College Corps. I am currently working as a Substitute Career Center Specialist doing more staff related tasks.
